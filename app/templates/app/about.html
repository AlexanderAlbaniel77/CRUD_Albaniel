About Page

Pinggang Pinoy is a web platform designed to share Filipino recipes, cooking tips, and techniques. Its goal is to help beginners, preserve traditional dishes, and support individuals who dream of starting small food businesses like karinderya. This project promotes Filipino food culture through accessible and easy-to-use technology.
